About

Hello, I am Dhaval Parmar. I love mathematics and it is amazing to see how every aspect of the physical world can be converted to numbers which can help understand the world better.

I am a Data Scientist working at ConcertAI which is contributing to improve the treatment of cancer patients. I did my Master of Technology in Artificial Intelligence (AI) from Indian Institute of Science (IISc), Bangalore.

My native place is a small town Anjar in Gujarat, India. After the earthquake in 2001, I can recollect we used to study in a school made out of tents. From those tents to IISc, It was a great journey. I am really excited about recent progress and research in the machine learning domain. My interest is a bit inclined toward NLP and Large Language Models (LLMs). I am exploring more about capabilities of LLMs and getting maximum out of them using different techniques. I am learning about implementation, testing and monitoring of ML models in production (MLops). You can learn more about my technical experience and achievements in Resume section.

I have worked on various kinds of projects in different areas. All project details and codes are available on GitHub repositories. I have also started writing technical blogs based on learning and explorations. You can read them in Blog section or check my Medium profile.


Photography is one of my passion, You can enjoy my clicks in the gallery. I also like gazing at stars and know more about the infinite universe. Recently, I have started learning piano and also want to learn flute.

I am collecting ideas for open source project to help specially abled people with the power of AI and other latest technologies. Please ping me, If you have any ideas or want to collaborate with me on the same project.

Always open to learn and collaborate. If you want to know more about me or want to connect feel free to mail me.
